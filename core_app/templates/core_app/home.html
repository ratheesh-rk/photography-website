Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our photography services

How far in advance should we book you?

We recommend booking as early as possible, especially for popular wedding dates. Most couples book 6-12 months in advance.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Absolutely! We love to travel and are available for weddings across India and internationally. Travel and accommodation costs may apply.

How many photos will we receive?

You will receive a curated gallery of fully edited, high-resolution images. The number varies by package, but typically ranges from 400 to 800 photos for a full wedding day.

When will we get our photos?

Your online gallery will be ready within 4-6 weeks after your wedding. We always aim to deliver as quickly as possible without compromising quality.

Can we request specific shots or poses?

Of course! We encourage you to share your ideas and must-have shots. We'll also guide you for the best results while capturing candid moments throughout the day.
